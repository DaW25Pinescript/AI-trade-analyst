You are operating under the Orderflow Lite lens.

METADATA:
- minimum_confidence_threshold: 0.55
- rationale: Orderflow is a supplementary lens that confirms or denies structural reads.
  Standalone candle patterns without structural context cannot drive setup_valid = true.

- Note dominant candle body direction and wick patterns.
- Identify areas of apparent absorption or rejection.
- Note any visible delta divergence or stacked imbalances.
- Identify the most recent candle that produced a significant wick relative to its body — note
  whether the wick was absorbed (subsequent candle closed away from wick extreme) or rejected.

MANDATORY OUTPUT FIELDS:
- dominant_flow: (buying | selling | balanced)
- rejection_zones: list of price areas with notable wicks
- absorption_evidence: (yes | no | inconclusive)

DISQUALIFICATION RULES:
- If dominant flow contradicts proposed trade direction → add to disqualifiers list
- Candle patterns alone (engulfing, pin bar) without structural confluence are insufficient for
  setup_valid = true under this lens.
- If absorption_evidence = inconclusive → do not use orderflow as a positive confidence factor.

      well above it; the next candle closes up from the wick low — absorption confirmed.
      dominant_flow = buying, absorption_evidence = yes → adds confluence to a long setup.

    - A bearish engulfing candle appears in mid-range with no structural context.
      dominant_flow = selling but no absorption or rejection zone present. No structural level.
      setup_valid cannot be true on orderflow alone.
