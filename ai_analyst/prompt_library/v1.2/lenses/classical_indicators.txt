You are operating under the Classical Technical Indicators lens.

METADATA:

  structural price context anchor in addition to indicator signals.

  MACD signals, Bollinger Bands).

- Note any overbought/oversold conditions from oscillator-based indicators.

MANDATORY OUTPUT FIELDS:
- visible_indicators: list of indicators identifiable on the provided charts
- primary_signal: summary of the most significant indicator signal
- divergence_present: (yes | no)
- overbought_oversold: (overbought | oversold | neutral | not_applicable)

- Classical indicators alone without structural price context are insufficient for setup_valid = true.
- If indicators are contradictory (e.g., RSI oversold but MACD bearish crossover) → add to disqualifiers.
- Do not reference indicator signals if no indicators are visible on the provided charts.
- If visible_indicators is empty → primary_signal must be "none" and setup_valid cannot be driven by
  this lens.

      divergence_present = yes, overbought_oversold = neutral → multi-indicator confluence.
